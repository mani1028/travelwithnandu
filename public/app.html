Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect your phone number for authentication and your name for profile creation. We also collect trip details (pickup/drop locations, dates) to facilitate your bookings.

2. How We Use It

Your data is used solely for booking management and driver coordination. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Terms & Conditions

1. Booking Services

Move With Nandu acts as an aggregator connecting passengers with drivers. While we strive for reliability, we are not liable for driver conduct or vehicle conditions.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 30 minutes before departure are free. Late cancellations may incur a fee at the driver's discretion.

3. Payment

All fares are estimates. Final fare is agreed with the driver. Online payment integration is coming soon.
