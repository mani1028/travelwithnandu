Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your Name and Mobile Number strictly to facilitate ride bookings and driver coordination. We do not store sensitive payment information on our servers.

2. Data Sharing

Your contact information is shared only with the driver assigned to your specific booking to allow them to coordinate pickup. We do not sell your data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. Location Data

We utilize location data only when you manually select pickup/drop points. We do not track your device location in the background when the app is not in use.
