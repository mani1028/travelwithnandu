Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect your phone number for authentication and your name for profile creation. We also collect trip details (pickup/drop locations, dates) to facilitate your bookings.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for coordinating rides with drivers and communicating booking status updates. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Location Data

We do not track your real-time location in the background. Location data is only used when you explicitly select pickup/drop points.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information stored on our servers.

5. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, please contact our support team via WhatsApp.

Terms & Conditions

1. Booking Services

Nandu Travels acts as an aggregator connecting passengers with drivers. While we strive for reliability, we are not liable for driver conduct or vehicle conditions.

2. Cancellations

Cancellations made within 1 hour of the trip may be subject to a cancellation fee. Repeated cancellations may result in account suspension.

3. User Conduct

Users are expected to behave respectfully towards drivers. Any report of misconduct may lead to a permanent ban.

4. Payments

Payments are typically made directly to the driver or via the app's payment gateway (if enabled). Prices shown are estimates and may vary slightly based on actual distance.

5. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance.
